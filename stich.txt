MO TA SITE CHUC NANG GIAO VIEN
Tai lieu yeu cau giao dien mau tren Figma
Pham vi: Khu vuc giao vien cua he thong thi truc tuyen co giam sat gian lan

1. Tong quan san pham
Khu vuc giao vien la workspace chinh de giao vien quan ly lop hoc, tao va xuat ban bai thi, theo doi ca thi truc tuyen, ra soat canh bao gian lan, xem ket qua va cap nhat ho so. Giao dien mau can di theo huong Modern SaaS: sang, toi gian, nhieu khoang tho vua du, nhan manh vao kha nang scan thong tin va thao tac nhanh.
Muc tieu thiet ke
•	Giao vien nhin thay viec can lam ngay khi vao dashboard: bai thi dang mo, ca giam sat, lop hoc, canh bao.
•	Cac thao tac lap lai nhu tao bai thi, vao phong giam sat, xem ket qua, quan ly lop phai ro rang va it buoc.
•	Giao dien uu tien tinh on dinh, doc du lieu tot, khong dung hero marketing hay trang tri qua day.
•	Thiet ke phai responsive cho desktop, laptop, tablet va mobile.
Phong cach hinh anh
Thanh phan	Dinh huong Figma
Mau nen	Light-first: nen xam rat nhat, surface trang, border xam xanh nhe.
Mau chinh	Indigo/blue dung cho CTA, active nav, focus ring va lien ket quan trong.
Trang thai	Success xanh, warning vang/cam, danger do, info xanh duong. Risk cao phai noi bat nhung khong gay roi.
Typography	Sans-serif hien dai, uu tien do doc tieng Viet. Heading dam vua phai, khong dung serif editorial.
Bo cuc	Sidebar trai + topbar + content max-width. Card radius 12-16px, khong long card trong card neu khong can.
Icon	Dung icon line style dong bo cho nav, action, status, empty state.

3. Khung layout dung chung
3.1 Sidebar
•	Vi tri ben trai, co logo EduExam, role Giao vien, danh sach nav: Tong quan, Lop hoc, Bai thi, Giam sat, Ho so.
•	Active state ro: nen primary-soft, text primary, co border/subtle inset.
•	Hover state nhe, khong dich chuyen manh lam roi mat.
•	Mobile/tablet: sidebar chuyen thanh top nav hoac drawer, khong che noi dung.
3.2 Topbar
•	Hien thi ten nguoi dung, workspace label va nut Dang xuat.
•	Co the mo rong sau nay cho search/toast/notification, nhung mau Figma phase dau chi can gon.
3.3 Page header
•	Moi man hinh co eyebrow, title, description ngan va action zone ben phai hoac ben duoi tren mobile.
•	Title khong qua lon nhu landing page; day la workspace van hanh.
•	CTA chinh luon dung primary button; action phu dung secondary/quiet button.
3.4 Component patterns
Pattern	Mo ta
Metric card	Label uppercase nho, value lon, hint ngan. Dung cho KPI dashboard va monitoring.
Action card	Dung trong quick actions, gom title + description ngan, click den route lien quan.
Data table	Header nen xam nhat, row hover primary-soft, link title mau primary.
Panel	Surface trang, border nhe, shadow mem, padding 24px.
Status chip	Pill nho co tone: success, warning, danger, info, neutral.
Empty state	Border dashed, message ngan, co CTA neu can.
Form section	Label dam, input cao 42px, focus ring primary, action bar can phai.

4. Dashboard giao vien
Muc tieu
Dashboard la man hinh dau tien sau khi giao vien dang nhap. No phai tra loi nhanh: hom nay giao vien co bao nhieu lop, bao nhieu bai thi, bai thi nao dang mo, co ca nao can giam sat, va nen bam vao dau tiep theo.
Bo cuc de ve Figma
1.	Page header: eyebrow "Giao vien", title "Tong quan lop hoc va bai thi", description ngan, action "Tao bai thi" va "Quan ly lop".
2.	Hero cong viec: panel ngang gom cau mo ta ngan + CTA Tao bai thi.
3.	Metric grid 4 cot desktop: Lop hoc, Bai thi, Sap dien ra, Giam sat.
4.	Quick actions: 4 card: Tao bai thi, Quan ly bai thi, Lop hoc, Giam sat.
5.	Content grid: ben trai Bai thi gan day, ben phai Ca giam sat.
Du lieu hien thi
Thanh phan	Du lieu
Lop hoc	So luong tu listClasses()
Bai thi	So luong tu listExams()
Sap dien ra	Bai thi active co startTime lon hon hien tai
Giam sat	So luong tu listMonitoringExams()
Bai thi gan day	Title, className, durationMinutes, status
Ca giam sat	Title/name, code/className, link vao monitoring

Trang thai can thiet ke
•	Loading: skeleton metric va panel hoac loading state gon.
•	Empty: chua co bai thi, chua co ca giam sat.
•	Error: panel loi co message tu API, co the them nut Thu lai.
•	Responsive: metric 4 cot -> 2 cot -> 1 cot; content grid 2 cot -> 1 cot.
5. Quan ly lop hoc
Man hinh danh sach lop
•	Muc tieu: giao vien xem cac lop dang quan ly va truy cap nhanh vao chi tiet lop.
•	Header: title "Danh sach lop hoc", action "Tao bai thi cho lop".
•	Metrics: so lop, tong hoc sinh, trung binh hoc sinh/lop neu co du lieu.
•	Noi dung chinh: table hoac card list hien thi ten lop, mon, ma lop, so hoc sinh.
•	Click ten lop de vao /teacher/class/:id.
Chi tiet lop
•	Header: ten lop va mo ta lop.
•	Panel thong tin lop: ma lop, mon, so hoc sinh, ngay cap nhat neu co.
•	Danh sach bai thi lien quan den lop: title, trang thai, thoi luong, link chi tiet bai thi.
•	Empty state: lop chua co bai thi dang mo.
6. Quan ly bai thi
Danh sach bai thi
•	Muc tieu: giao vien xem toan bo bai thi, trang thai va truy cap nhanh den chi tiet.
•	Header: title "Tat ca bai thi cua giao vien", CTA "Soan bai thi".
•	Metrics: tong bai thi, dang mo, sap dien ra, nhap/da ket thuc neu co du lieu.
•	Table columns: Bai thi, Lop, Phut, Trang thai.
•	Status chips: Nhap warning, Dang mo success, Sap dien ra info, Da ket thuc neutral.
Chi tiet bai thi
•	Hien thi thong tin tong quan bai thi: title, duration, active status, className/classId.
•	Danh sach cau hoi: noi dung, loai cau hoi, do kho/trang thai neu backend tra ve.
•	Danh sach giao bai/assignments: title, published status.
•	Action co the co trong mau: Quay lai danh sach, Mo giam sat, Xem ra soat ket qua.
7. Tao bai thi / Exam build
Muc tieu
Trong demo_2 hien tai, form tao bai thi duoc giu toi gian de dam bao on dinh contract classId. Mau Figma nen the hien duoc workspace tao nhanh va co the mo rong thanh wizard sau nay.
Bo cuc
6.	Header: eyebrow "Soan bai", title "Tao bai thi rieng tu bang classId".
7.	Panel form chinh: Ten bai thi, Lop hoc, Thoi luong phut.
8.	Action bar: Xem danh sach, Tao bai thi bang classId.
9.	Preview phu neu can: ten bai thi, lop, thoi luong, trang thai Nhap.
Trang thai va validation
•	Neu chua nhap ten bai thi hoac chua chon lop, CTA tao co the disabled hoac show error inline.
•	Khi dang tao: button loading "Dang xu ly".
•	Sau khi tao thanh cong: redirect den route created-success/chi tiet voi examId.
•	Khi loi API: error panel hoac toast.
8. Giam sat thi truc tuyen
Danh sach ca giam sat
•	Route /teacher/live-monitoring hien thi cac bai thi co the giam sat.
•	Table columns: Bai thi, Lop/ma, thoi gian, trang thai.
•	Click vao bai thi de mo /teacher/exams/:examId/monitoring.
Phong giam sat bai thi
•	Muc tieu: giao vien scan nhanh so luong thi sinh, rui ro, trang thai nop bai va mo chi tiet hoc sinh.
•	Header: ten bai thi, mo ta monitoring/proctor API, action Doi ca thi.
•	Metric strip: Bai thi, Phien lam bai, Thoi luong.
•	Table: Hoc sinh, Trang thai, Rui ro, Nop bai.
•	Risk score chip: success neu thap, warning neu trung binh, danger neu cao.
•	Panel phu: Trang thai phong thi, canh bao, camera frame.
Chi tiet hoc sinh trong phien thi
•	Header: title "Chi tiet hoc sinh trong phien thi".
•	Action buttons: Canh bao, Tam dung, Tiep tuc, Huy bai.
•	Metrics: Attempt ID, Rui ro, Canh bao.
•	Table timeline: Su kien, Loai, Trang thai, Thoi diem.
•	Panel phu: Bai thi, canh bao, camera frame moi nhat.
Trang thai can thiet ke
Trang thai	Y nghia UI
ONLINE/ACTIVE/IN_PROGRESS	Chip success, uu tien hien thi tren bang.
PAUSED/PENDING	Chip warning, co action Tiep tuc neu duoc phep.
SUBMITTED/COMPLETED	Chip info/neutral, khong cho thao tac nguy hiem.
STOPPED/INVALIDATED/FAILED	Chip danger, can doc ro ly do neu co.
Risk >= 70	Danger chip, nen sap xep uu tien cao.
Risk 35-69	Warning chip.
Risk < 35	Success chip.

9. Ra soat ket qua va canh bao
•	Route /teacher/exams/review/summary dung de xem canh bao gian lan va attempts cua bai thi.
•	Header: "Ra soat bai thi va su co".
•	Neu co examId: hien thi danh sach warnings + attempts.
•	Neu khong co examId: hien thi danh sach bai thi de giao vien chon.
•	Table columns: Muc ra soat, Rui ro, Trang thai, Thoi diem.
•	Figma nen co filter bar tuong lai: tat ca, can xu ly, da xac nhan, bo qua.
10. Ho so giao vien
Muc tieu
Trang ho so cho phep giao vien xem va cap nhat thong tin ca nhan. Phase demo_2 tap trung vao cac field co san trong API profile.
Thanh phan
•	Header: title "Thong tin giao vien".
•	Form fields: Ten hien thi, Ho ten, Email, So dien thoai.
•	Action: Luu ho so.
•	Success state: status chip "Da luu ho so".
•	Error state: toast hoac error panel khi API fail.
11. He thong trang thai chung
Loai state	Mo ta Figma
Loading	Dung skeleton hoac panel loading gon, khong lam layout nhay manh.
Empty	Khung dashed nhe, title ngan, message huong dan tiep theo.
Error	Panel co tone danger, message tu API, nen co nut Thu lai neu la list page.
Success	Toast hoac chip success, text ngan.
Disabled	Opacity 58%, cursor not-allowed, khong mat text contrast qua nhieu.
Focus	Ring primary 3px, bat buoc ro cho keyboard users.
